The kind of light I shoot for

More on Instagram @aeva_arte

Not a tour. A walk with a photographer's eye.

I'm the Japanese photographer behind aeva. I shoot Japan's landscapes, streets and famous places for the atmosphere they carry. On a walk, that eye is yours: where to stand, and when the light turns.

  • Iconic spots, perfectly timed: golden hour, blue hour, neon
  • Natural, editorial photos. You don't need to pose well
  • Relaxed pace, friendly, zero pressure
  • Language is no problem: translation apps plus simple guidance

Good to know

Do you speak English?

Basic English, plus a translation app for anything more. Booking is by text, so we can take our time. The photos do most of the talking.

Which areas do you cover? Where do we meet?

Currently available in Tokyo, Kanagawa, Nagoya and Osaka. We meet at a station or landmark near the spot we choose. I'll share the exact meeting point and a map after booking.

How many photos do I get, and when?

Roughly 30, 60 or 100 edited photos depending on the plan, delivered in a private online gallery within one week.

Can I use the photos on social media?

Yes. They're yours to share personally. Please tag @aeva_arte if you like them.

What if it rains?

We reschedule for free, or switch to covered and indoor-friendly spots. Your call.

How do I pay?

Secure online payment (card, incl. Alipay and WeChat Pay) to confirm your booking. Details are sent when you book.
